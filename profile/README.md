# Lucyn

**The institutional memory of your company.**

Lucyn turns scattered company knowledge into a living, connected map
of how your company actually works.

Companies lose knowledge constantly — when people leave, when decisions go undocumented, when context lives only in someone's head. Lucyn ingests GitHub, Discord, and meetings to build a structured knowledge graph written as human-readable Markdown, making every service, decision, and expert queryable in plain English. Ask anything about your company and get a grounded, cited answer in seconds.

## How it works

**Ingests** — GitHub, Discord, and Google Meet, continuously.

**Maps** — Builds a structured knowledge graph written as
human-readable Markdown in an Obsidian-compatible vault.

**Answers** — Ask anything about your company in plain English.
Get a grounded, cited answer in seconds.
