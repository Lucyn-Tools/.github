# Lucyn

**The institutional memory of your company.**

Lucyn turns scattered company knowledge into a living, connected map
of how your company actually works — searchable, queryable, and actionable.

---

Companies lose knowledge constantly — when people leave, when decisions go
undocumented, when context lives only in someone's head.

Lucyn ingests GitHub, Discord, and meetings to build a structured knowledge
graph of how your company actually works. Every service, decision, and expert
queryable in plain English. Ask anything and get a grounded, cited answer
in seconds.

---

**What exists** — Every service, module, and system your company has built,
mapped and documented automatically.

**Who knows what** — The real knowledge owners of every part of your product,
inferred from behavior — not job titles.

**What was decided** — Every decision, tradeoff, and open question captured
from meetings and discussions, never lost again.
